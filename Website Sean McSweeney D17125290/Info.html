Info

The Burger Joint story began in 1990, when the first TBJ store in Dublin, Ireland was opened. A year later, business was booming and over 50 stores was opened around Ireland. The business idea was simple, to deliver hot, freshly made food in as quick a time as safely possible, while playing an active role in the local community. Through a little known format now called franchising, other people invested in opening their own stores. Now with over 600 restaurants in Ireland alone The Burger Joint is a well known place to "grab a burger with the boys".
